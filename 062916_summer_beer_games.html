Summer Beer Games

29 June 2016
It is officially summer! The barbeques have started, the beachgoers have made their pilgrimage, and the beer games have made their way into every backyard party. It doesn’t matter if it’s a family party, frat party, or a hangout at the beach, beer games are a necessity. There’s nothing better than hanging with friends all day in the 80 degree weather drinking beers, grilling, and chilling. Here are the top beer games to have at your next get-together.

Spike Ball

Although it is nearly impossible to play this one with a beer in your hand, you can definitely try. Two teams play against each other, taking turns spiking the ball into the trampoline, making it hard for the opponent to return the ball. Definitely a fast-paced, intense game. Try it while holding a beer, or just make the loser chug? Can’t lose when there’s beer involved!

The Manhattan

image by dishmaps.com

Ingredients:

2 oz. Bourbon
1 oz. Sweet Vermouth
2 dashes Angostura bitters

Garnish:

Cherry

Directions:

Stir ingredients in a glass with ice, strain into a chilled glass.

Americano Cocktail

Ingredients:

1 ½ oz. Campari
1 ½ oz. sweet Vermouth
Club Soda

Garnish:

Orange Slice

Directions:

Pour the Campari and Vermouth in an ice-filled glass. Top with soda and stir.
image by tastespotting.com

Whiskey Smash

image by recipeshubs.com

Ingredients:

2 oz. Bourbon
.75 oz. Simple syrup
6 Mint leaves
Half a lemon cut into wedges

Garnish:

Mint

Directions:

Muddle lemon wedges, mint, and simple syrup in a shaker. Add ice and bourbon and shake vigorously. Strain over crushed ice.

Not Your Father’s Root Beer Float

Ingredients:

1 Not Your Father’s Root Beer
1 Scoop of Ice Cream (Vanilla Based)
1 Shot of Rum Chata

Directions:

Put one scoop of ice cream in a pint glass and add the shot of Rum Chata. Pour Not My Father’s Root Beer over the ice cream.
