Punch for ‘Murica

2 July 2016
Happy Birthday America! Celebrate your 4th of July weekend with one of these fabulous patriotic punch recipes! Surrounding yourself with friends, family, barbeque, and superb cocktails is what this long weekend is for! So have your friends over and supply them with these wonderful jugs of punch to keep them cool in the blazing sun. Happy Fourth!

Mint Julep Margarita

Ingredients:

2 limes
2 tsp. superfine sugar
10 fresh mint leaves
2 oz. bourbon
1 oz. orange liqueur
¼ C. Seltzer
image by thedish.restaurant.com

Directions:

Place cut up limes, sugar, and mint leaves in cocktail shaker to muddle. Add the bourbon, orange liquor and a couple ice cubes. Shake well and strain into glass. Can be served over ice or straight. Top with seltzer and garnish with a lime wedge.

Chile-Agave Margarita

image by rachaelrayshow.com

Ingredients:

1 Tbsp. turbinado sugar
1 tsp orange rind
1/8 tsp kosher salt
8 thin slices of chile
2 Tbsp. lime juice 1 Tbsp. fresh orange juice
1 Tbsp. light agave nectar
2 oz. anejo tequila
1 oz. Grand Marnier

Directions:

Combine the sugar, orange rind, and salt on a plate. Moisten rims and dip rims in plate to coat. Place 4 chile slices in shaker to muddle. Add remaining ingredients and fill shaker with ice. Strain into prepared glasses, and add chile slices for garnish.

Watermelon Margarita

Ingredients:

2 tsp sugar
1 lime wedge
3 ½ C. cubed seeded watermelon
½ C. tequila
2 Tbsp. Sugar
3 Tbsp. lime juice
1 Tbsp. Triple Sec
Lime wedges or watermelon balls
image by sagerecipes.com

Directions:

Rub rims of 6 glasses with a lime wedge and rub the rims in the 2 Tbsp. of sugar. Combine the watermelon, tequila, sugar, lime juice, and triple sec in a blender. Blend till smooth. Fill glasses with crushed ice and add margarita. Garnish with lime wedges and melon balls.

Prickly Pear Margarita

Image by flipmyfood.com

Ingredients:

1 lime wedge
3 C. fresh lime juice
3 C. Silver Tequila
2 C. prickly pear syrup
1 C. orange liqueur
Coarse pink salt for rimming glasses

Directions:

Place the pink salt in a plate and rim glasses with it using a lime wedge. Fill a pitcher with ice and add lime juice, tequila, prickly pear syrup, and orange liqueur. Stir together and strain into glasses. Makes 10 cocktails.

French Margarita

Ingredients:

2 oz. tequila
1 oz. Grand Marnier
½ oz. of lime juice, orange juice, and sweet and sour mix
1 oz. Chambord
Garnish with berries
image by thellosonoma.com

Directions:

Pour tequila, Grand Marnier, lime juice, orange juice, and sour mix into a shaker and shake to mix. Fill a margarita glass with ice and strain the mixture into the glass. Top with Chambord and garnish with a skewer of berries.
