NYC Craft Beer Festival

3 June 2016

Friday June 17th and Saturday June 18th New York City will be hosting their big Craft Beer Festival at the Lexington Avenue Armory. There will be 75 breweries - 150 beers, ciders, and meads! There will be informative seminars and one-on-one meet and greets with Cicerones in the Craft Concierge Center. This is a great opportunity to discover what type of craft beer you truly enjoy and what the process of making it is. There will also be a Sense Lab which will help you determine what flavors you enjoy most in your beer!

Friday hours are from 8Pm-11PM and VIP ticket holders start at 7Pm. On Saturday, hours are from 2Pm-5PM with VIP starting at 1PM and a second session from 7PM-10PM with VIP starting at 6PM. All VIP members get an extra hour of tastings and also get to try 150 extra beers that general admission does not have access to.
