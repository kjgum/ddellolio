Drinks for Dad

17 June 2016
This Sunday is a day to celebrate the love between father and child…And there’s no better way to say, “I love you Dad, Happy Father’s Day” then with bourbon! Leave the fancy umbrella toothpicks, pineapples, and flowers for another day, and pick up the beer and scotch. Wow your father with some of these stiff cocktails to make a Father’s Day he will remember…Or, not!

The Roosevelt Cocktail

Ingredients:

1.75 oz. Dark Rum
.5 oz. Dry Vermouth
.25 oz. Fresh Orange Juice
.25 tsp Sugar

Garnish:

1 Orange Twist

Directions:

Fill shaker with the rum, Vermouth, orange juice, sugar, and ice. Shake, and strain into chilled cocktail glass. Garnish with a twist!

The Manhattan

image by dishmaps.com

Ingredients:

2 oz. Bourbon
1 oz. Sweet Vermouth
2 dashes Angostura bitters

Garnish:

Cherry

Directions:

Stir ingredients in a glass with ice, strain into a chilled glass.

Americano Cocktail

Ingredients:

1 ½ oz. Campari
1 ½ oz. sweet Vermouth
Club Soda

Garnish:

Orange Slice

Directions:

Pour the Campari and Vermouth in an ice-filled glass. Top with soda and stir.

Whiskey Smash

image by recipeshubs.com

Ingredients:

2 oz. Bourbon
.75 oz. Simple syrup
6 Mint leaves
Half a lemon cut into wedges

Garnish:

Mint

Directions:

Muddle lemon wedges, mint, and simple syrup in a shaker. Add ice and bourbon and shake vigorously. Strain over crushed ice.

Not Your Father’s Root Beer Float

Ingredients:

1 Not Your Father’s Root Beer
1 Scoop of Ice Cream (Vanilla Based)
1 Shot of Rum Chata

Directions:

Put one scoop of ice cream in a pint glass and add the shot of Rum Chata. Pour Not My Father’s Root Beer over the ice cream.
