Margaritas on my Mind

27 July 2016
I don’t always drink Tequila…But when I do, it’s in a margarita. Fruity margaritas, to spicy margaritas, on the rocks, or frozen, the summer sun puts me in a margarita mindset. Here is a list of some of the greatest margarita recipes for your next get together. My favorite is a combo of the fruity and spicy margarita. In Belmar, New Jersey at the 10th Ave. Burrito they have a Spicy Pineapple Margarita with El Jimador Reposado, Pineapple, Habanero, Lime, and cane sugar. One of my favorites!
